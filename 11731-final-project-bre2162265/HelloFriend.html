Welcome to Hello Friend

In a world where technology rules our daily lives, it is easy to feel lonely. Whether you have lost track of old friends, moved to a new place, or simply miss having a conversation, you have found a place to have meaningful, genuine interactions in a safe environment. At Hello Friend our volunteers are ready to spend quality time doing things like:

  • Meet with friendly people who share your interests and values.
  • Join group chats or one-on-one conversations at your own pace.
  • Share stories, hobbies, and experiences in a judgment-free environment.
  • Find support and companionship when you need it most.
  • Share dinner or see a movie
  • Participate in group events

No matter where you are in life, there is spot for you here. So, relax, have a seat, and start a conversation with:

Hello Friend.
