Purpose Statement

Hello Friend is a safe, welcoming space where people can connect, share, and build genuine friendships. Our purpose is simple: make companionship accessible to everyone through kindness, inclusion, and meaningful conversations.

Privacy Policy

Hello Friend uses the information you provide to create and manage your account, connect you with appropriate companions, improve our website, and ensure safety. We do not sell your information. We only share your personal information with providers that help run the site and as required by law. This site is intended for adults 18+ who experience loneliness.
